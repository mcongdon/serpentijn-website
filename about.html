All About Us

Our Purpose

Serpentijn Art & Athletics is creativity serving something bigger than commerce. It draws on our love of design, art, and cycling, and our wide network of talented friends, to create beautiful things that help people live better lives.

Nonprofit Partners

10% of each purchase you make goes to a worthy non-profit organization, selected by you during checkout. For 2016, your choice of charities includes:

The Dreaming Zebra Foundation

The Dreaming Zebra Foundation is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit encouraging children to embrace their individuality, to express themselves creatively, and follow their artistic dreams. Your gifts help us provide art & music supplies to underprivileged children throughout the world. www.dreamingzebra.org

Team Africa Rising

Team Africa Rising's mission is to unify African countries through the sport of cycling. Beginning in Rwanda, a country torn apart by genocide in 1994, we have assembled a team of professional cyclists who now race competitively on the international stage and serve as ambassadors of hope. www.teamafricarising.org

Artists

Keith Negley

Keith has been working for over 15 years as a freelance illustrator. His clients include a wide range of major newspapers, national magazines and publishers and he is a frequent contributor to the New York Times and New Yorker. He received his BFA from the Milwaukee Institute of Art and Design in 2000 and his MFA from The School of Visual Arts in New York in 2013. He's been honored with recognition from the Society of Illustrators with 3 silver medals in addition to being on the jury, as well as 2 bronze medals from 3X3, and the New York Times "Best of OpEd Art." He recently published his first children's book for Flying Eye Books, Tough Guys Have Feelings Too, and is an adjunct professor of professional practices at Emily Carr University in Vancouver, British Columbia. Keith resides in the mountains of Bellingham, Washington, with his wife and 7-year-old hellspawn. KeithNegley.com

Barbra Ignatiev

A Wisconsin native now based in Oakland, California, Barbra creates watercolor prints and patterns for her brand Barbarian. The spirit of Barbarian encompasses a deep desire for a brave and colorful life of freedom filled with nature, music and travel. Her intent is to inspire adventurousness and soul-searching in others. BarbraIgnatiev.com
